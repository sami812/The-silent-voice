### **1. Functional Requirements**

1. The app shall **convert spoken language into written text**.
    
2. The app shall **convert written text into speech**.
    
3. The app shall **provide context-based response** suggestions to the user.
    
4. The user shall be able to select a suggested response or write a custom text response.
    
5. The app shall **store basic personal information** (e.g., name, address) locally for convenience.
    
6. The app shall allow offline operation for TTS, STT, and lightweight NLP.
    
7. The app shall allow users to view conversation history locally.
    
8. The app shall support cross-platform mobile deployment (Android and iOS).
    
---

### **2. Non-Functional Requirements**

1. The app shall process speech-to-text and text-to-speech within 2 seconds per interaction.
    
2. The app shall operate on mid-range smartphones without noticeable lag.
    
3. The app shall store all user data locally to ensure privacy.
    
4. The app shall provide a simple and intuitive UI for users.
    
5. The app shall follow consistent coding and design standards across the team.
	
---

### **3. User Requirements**

1. Users should be able to speak and see their words converted into text immediately.
    
2. Users should be able to read suggested responses and select or modify them easily.
    
3. Users should be able to hear responses from the app in clear, natural speech.
    
4. Users should be able to save their basic personal info for faster usage.
    
---

### **4. System Requirements**

1. The system must work offline for core functionalities (STT, TTS, NLP).
    
2. The system must operate within the storage and memory limits of a standard mobile device.
    
---

### **5. Performance Requirements**

1. The app shall process speech-to-text in less than 2 seconds.
    
2. The app shall generate response suggestions within 1–2 seconds.
    
---

### **6. Security Requirements**

1. Personal information shall be encrypted when stored on the device.
    
2. The app shall not transmit personal data to any external servers.
    
3. The app shall prevent unauthorized access to stored user data.
    
---

### **7. Usability Requirements**

1. The interface shall be simple and accessible for all age groups.
    
2. Error messages shall be clear and provide guidance for next steps.
    
---

### **8. Reliability & Availability Requirements**

1. The app shall operate without crashing during normal use.
    
2. The app shall recover gracefully from temporary failures (e.g., app restart).
    
---

### **9. Compatibility Requirements**

1. The app shall be compatible with Android and iOS devices.
    
2. The app shall integrate with device microphones, speakers, and local storage.
    
---

### **10. Regulatory & Compliance Requirements**

1. The app shall store sensitive user data only locally, with no cloud transmission.

---

### **11. Documentation Requirements**

1. Technical documentation shall be maintained for all modules (STT, TTS, NLP, UI).
    
2. User manuals shall be provided to explain how to use the app’s features.
    
3. Change logs shall be maintained for all updates.
    
---

### **12. Interface Requirements**

1. The app shall use the device microphone for speech input.
    
2. The app shall use the device speaker for speech output.
    
3. The app shall provide a touch-based UI compatible with mobile screens.
    
---

### **13. Data Requirements**

1. User personal information shall be stored securely on the device.
    
2. Backup and export of conversation history are out-of-scope.
    
---

### **14. Legal & Contractual Requirements**

1. The development team shall comply with open-source licensing for any libraries used (e.g., STT/TTS libraries).
    
2. The app shall respect intellectual property rights for all content and algorithms used.
